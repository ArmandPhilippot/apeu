## Usage

This component let you toggle the visibility of its contents by clicking on its label.

<Collapsible>
  <span slot="label">A label</span>
  The collapsible body
</Collapsible>

## Using <code>isProse</code>

<Collapsible isProse>
  <span slot="label">A label</span>
  The collapsible body
</Collapsible>
