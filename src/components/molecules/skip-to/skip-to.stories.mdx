## Usage

A SkipLink component is special. It is used to navigate through a page using anchors.

Click here then use <kbd>Tab</kbd> key to focus the skip link below. It should then appears on the top left of your screen.

<SkipTo anchor="#some-id">Skip to some anchor on the page</SkipTo>
